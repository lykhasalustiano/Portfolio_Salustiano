About Me

I have recently graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from the College of Mary Immaculate. My education provided me with an excellent basis in the principles of computer science, but it did more than just that; it made me develop a great interest in the design aspect.

In the process of obtaining my degree, I conducted my On-the-Job Training at JCAS Logistics, which gave me the opportunity to put my theoretical knowledge into practice and gain practical experience. What is more interesting is that this experience turned out to be quite positive, and consequently, I was offered a part-time job there, which allowed me to combine working with studying. In such a way, I got the chance to familiarize myself with the operations in the logistics sphere and acquire experience as a working professional.

My skills include UI/UX Design, Figma, FlutterFlow Development, Balsamiq Wireframing, Prototyping, and UX Testing. When designing the interface of any product, I like creating clear and easily comprehensible designs for users. Now I would like to advance in my career path and take the next step.
